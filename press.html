Short Description

Fidel Family helps toddlers learn Tigrinya, Amharic, and English through play. Explore content packs, play matching games, and discover new words in three languages — all offline.

Long Description

Fidel Family is a language learning app designed for children ages 1 to 5 in the East African diaspora. Built for families who want their children to grow up bilingual (or trilingual), the app teaches Tigrinya, Amharic, and English through interactive content packs and a matching memory game.

Every word appears in Ge'ez script alongside Latin transliteration, making it natural for children to see and connect both writing systems. Content packs cover everyday categories like animals, colors, numbers, food, and family members.

The app is designed with privacy first: zero data collection, no ads, no tracking, and complete offline functionality. All content downloads with the app so children can learn anywhere — on a plane, in the car, or at home without Wi-Fi.

Fidel Family was created by Dino Yemane, an Eritrean-American developer, to help preserve East African languages for the next generation.
